Privacy Policy

Introduction

DDRC Mumbai is committed to protecting the privacy of its website visitors. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website.

Information We Collect

Automatically Collected Information

  • IP address and browser type
  • Pages visited and time spent on website
  • Referring website information
  • Device and operating system information

Voluntarily Provided Information

  • Name and contact details when you submit forms
  • Application details for UDID registration
  • Feedback and query submissions

How We Use Your Information

  • Process UDID applications and service requests
  • Respond to your queries and feedback
  • Improve website functionality and user experience
  • Generate anonymized statistical reports

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. All sensitive data is transmitted using SSL encryption.

Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties except:

  • When required by law or government authorities
  • With other government departments for service delivery
  • With service providers under confidentiality agreements

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data by contacting us at ddrcmumbai@gmail.com.
