About DDRC Mumbai

Empowering Persons with Disabilities since 1985

Who We Are

The District Disability Rehabilitation Centre (DDRC) Mumbai is a government initiative under the Department of Empowerment of Persons with Disabilities (DEPwD), Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India.

Our Mission

To facilitate the rehabilitation and empowerment of persons with disabilities by providing accessible, quality services and ensuring they can participate fully in society with dignity and independence.

Our Vision

An inclusive society where persons with disabilities have equal opportunities for education, employment, and social participation.

Our Services

  • Assessment & Early Intervention: Early identification for children with disabilities
  • Therapeutic Services: Physiotherapy, occupational therapy, speech therapy
  • Assistive Device Distribution: Wheelchairs, hearing aids, prosthetics
  • UDID Registration Support: Assistance with Unique Disability ID card
  • Skill Development: Vocational training and employment support
  • Counseling Services: Psychological support for persons with disabilities
  • Outreach Camps: Regular camps for service delivery

Legal Framework

  • Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016 (RPwD Act)
  • National Policy for Persons with Disabilities, 2006
  • UN Convention on the Rights of Persons with Disabilities (UNCRPD)
