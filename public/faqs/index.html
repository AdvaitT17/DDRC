Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about DDRC services, UDID registration, and disability certification

What is UDID and why is it important?

UDID (Unique Disability ID) is a government-issued identification card for persons with disabilities in India. It serves as a single document for identification, verification, and availing various benefits and schemes provided by the government.

How can I apply for UDID card through DDRC Mumbai?

You can apply by visiting DDRC Mumbai office, applying through our online portal, or attending one of our medical assessment camps. Our team will guide you through the registration process.

What documents are required for UDID application?

  • Passport size photograph
  • Proof of identity (Aadhaar Card, Voter ID, etc.)
  • Proof of address
  • Medical records related to disability
  • Income certificate (if applicable)

What services does DDRC Mumbai offer?

  • Assessment and early intervention services
  • Therapeutic services (physiotherapy, occupational therapy, speech therapy)
  • Assistive device distribution and fitting
  • Skill development and vocational training programs
  • Counseling for persons with disabilities and their families
  • UDID registration assistance

Are DDRC services free of cost?

Yes, most of the services provided at DDRC Mumbai are free of cost as they are funded by the Government of India. Some specialized services or assistive devices may have nominal charges based on income criteria.

What are the working hours of DDRC Mumbai?

  • Monday to Friday: 10:00 AM to 5:30 PM
  • Saturday: 10:00 AM to 1:00 PM (limited services)
  • Closed on Sundays and Government holidays

What government schemes are available for persons with disabilities?

  • Educational scholarships for students with disabilities
  • Assistive devices and artificial limbs provision
  • Financial assistance for rehabilitation
  • Self-employment seed capital scheme
  • Indira Gandhi National Disability Pension
  • Concessions in railway and ST bus fares

How can I avail these government schemes?

To avail government schemes, you need a valid disability certificate or UDID card. Visit DDRC Mumbai with your documents, and our counselors will help you identify eligible schemes and guide you through the application process.
